HTML Links and Navigation

What are Hypertext and Hyperlinks?

Hypertext

Hypertext is text that contains links to other texts, documents, or resources. It allows non-linear reading and navigation through information. The "HT" in HTML stands for HyperText.

Hyperlinks

Hyperlinks (or simply "links") are clickable elements that connect one resource to another. They are the foundation of the World Wide Web, allowing users to navigate between pages and resources.

Link States (Default Browser Styling)

Links have different states that browsers style by default:

  • Unvisited links: Usually blue and underlined
  • Visited links: Usually purple
  • Active links: Usually red (while being clicked)
  • Hover state: May change appearance when mouse hovers over
